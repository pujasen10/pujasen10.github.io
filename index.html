Our Story

Just yarn, patience
and a little creativity

The Loopy Looms was born from a deep love of crochet and a belief that handmade things carry something you can truly feel — a little piece of the maker in every loop, every knot, every tiny petal.

Every creation here takes its cue from the natural world: the delicate bell of a Lily of the Valley, the soft unfurling of a pastel bloom, the lushness of a garden coming to life. Flowers that are beautiful, but built to last forever.
